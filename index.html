The first name that was given to the drink was "Pemberton's French Wine Coca" Pemberton's was a variation of the so-called "coca wine" (or Vin Mariani ), a blend of wine and coca leaves that had enjoyed great success in Europe when it was created by the Corsican pharmacist Angelo Mariani. Sprite is a lemon - flavored , caffeine - free soft drink produced by The Coca -Cola Company . It is a soda from Germany , initially called Fanta Klare Zitrone ( clear Fanta with lemon); later, the branding was redesigned as "Sprite".

Fanta orange soda is available in over 180 countries. Fanta orange soda is more popular in Europe and South America than in the United States of America , and the products are formulated differently, with the European version containing real orange juice from local citrus fruits (mainly due to stricter legislation regarding "orange soda" denomination - which requires at least 20% real juice - and the laws on natural flavourings). In terms of volume, Brazil is the largest consumer of Fanta in the world.

Coca-Cola boasts several places entirely linked to the brand, including a museum in Atlanta , the company's headquarters, and some merchandising stores , the World of Coca-Cola in New York and Las Vegas . Water , carbon dioxide , sugar , caramel coloring (E 150D), natural flavors , caffeine and phosphoric acid (E 338).
